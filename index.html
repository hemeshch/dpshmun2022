Letter from Secretary General

Meher Nanda Pudi

Dear Prospective Delegates,
We are pleased to bring you another edition of the Delhi Public School Hyderabad Model United Nations. This year we bring to you six exciting committees which address issues all along the socio-economic and political spectrum.

We seek to bring out your hidden talents of crafting policy, public speaking and heated negotiating. Me and my team are working extremely hard to ensure that each one of you get the most out of your Model UN experience at DPS Hyderabad with an experienced executive board and exciting agendas you are sure to learn something new at this conference. We hope you all make countless memories that will get you all started on your own Model UN journey.

Looking forward to welcoming you all,
Meher Nanda Pudi
Secretary General
